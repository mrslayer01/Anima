Summoning mechanics
Summoning is split up between 4 abilities. Summon, Control, Bind and Banish. These are ably to achieve mastery and require at least 5 to be used.

Summoning uses MA to accumulate Zeon as per Magic but does not require The Gift. To summon/control/bind/banish a creature, the ability must succeed roll above the difficulty and have the required Zeon as specified in the Difficulty table (SUMMONING_DIFFICULTY_TABLE). 


There are two ways to use summoning abilities, instant or via a ritual.

Instant summoning is an active action, usually done in combat. You open roll with the chosen ability
